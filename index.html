Biography

Shubhankar holds the position of Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Engineering at Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar Technological University, Lonere. A dedicated researcher and educator, he specializes in the fields of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning, while actively exploring critical issues in cybersecurity, including ethical hacking and cyber defense mechanisms. Distinguishing himself from his peers, Shubhankar is also a "renowned artist" and professional instrumentalist, known for his performances on the Harmonium and Reed Organ.
